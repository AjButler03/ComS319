Cars Catalogue

C8 Corvette

Starting for the 2020 model year, the C8 Corvette is GM's attempt to enter the world of super cars. With a new mid-engine layout, it manages to make a decent entrance.

Mustang

As an american favorite, the new Mustang has big shoes to fill. With the regular Mustang formula of a big engine up front in a low two-door car left alone, it stays true to form.

Porsche

Porsche has always been a bit odd, coming from a redesigned beetle. But it's hard to deny that the engine being over the rear wheels makes a really fun time, and the newest in the lineup is no exception.

Forte

Well, it's a car. It has four wheels, an engine, some seats, and can be driven around. Can't ask for much more than that.
